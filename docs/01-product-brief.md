# OathFi Product Brief

---

## 1. Product Name

```text
OathFi
```

---

## 2. Product Positioning

OathFi is an AI-powered trading research and risk-control agent for the HTX ecosystem.

It connects market data, detects market events, generates structured trading hypotheses, validates hypotheses through backtesting, applies a risk firewall, and supports paper execution with full audit review.

OathFi is not a generic trading terminal, not an exchange, not a wallet, and not a chatbot.

---

## 3. One-line Description

```text
OathFi turns market events into verifiable, backtested, risk-controlled trading workflows.
```

Chinese version:

```text
OathFi 将市场异动转化为可验证、可回测、可风控、可复盘的交易研究流程。
```

## 4. Core Workflow

The product must always follow this workflow:

```text
HTX Market Data
→ Market Event Detection
→ AI Hypothesis Generation
→ Backtest Validation
→ Risk Firewall
→ Paper Execution
→ Audit Review
```

UI workflow stepper:

```text
Market Event → AI Hypothesis → Backtest → Risk Check → Paper Trade → Review
```

## 5. Product Goal

OathFi helps users answer:

```text
What happened in the market?
Why does it matter?
What trading hypothesis can be tested?
Does historical data support this hypothesis?
What are the risks?
Should this be paper-executed or rejected?
```

## 9. Hackathon Continuity Rule

OathFi must remain the same product from initial submission to final demo.

Do not change:

```text
Product name
Product direction
Core workflow
Primary pages
HTX ecosystem positioning
AI trading research + risk-control narrative
```

Allowed improvements:

```text
UI polish
Bug fixes
Performance
i18n completeness
Data integration depth
```

The application must include these pages only unless the user explicitly approves changes:

```text
Command Center
Market Monitor
Agent Lab
Backtest Studio
Risk Firewall
Paper Execution
Audit Reports
HTX Ecosystem
Settings
```

---

## 11. Page Purpose Summary

### Command Center

First screen. Must explain product value within 10 seconds.

### Market Monitor

Professional HTX market-data monitoring page with chart, order book, trades, indicators, and event timeline.

### Agent Lab

Structured AI hypothesis-generation workspace. Must not be a normal chat interface.

### Backtest Studio

Validates AI hypotheses with historical data, metrics, equity curve, drawdown, and trade list.

### Risk Firewall

Institutional-style risk review with rule-by-rule evaluation and paper-trading approval.

### Paper Execution

Simulated execution only. Must clearly show no real funds are involved.

### Audit Reports

Decision review and audit trail.

### HTX Ecosystem

Shows HTX API, AI compute layer, $HTX utility model, and future ecosystem roadmap.

### Settings

Data source, agent, risk, and demo mode settings.
